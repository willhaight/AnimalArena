Welcome to Animal Arena!

Choose your animal and defeat your opponent in single combat.
Instructions are at the bottom of the page.

Instructions

Choose your animal and select one of the three abilities that are available to to each animal. Each animal's Ablities are unique that animal. Choose your ability and then click the computers turn for the computer to choose their ability then repeat until the game is finished. Refresh the page to start again. If you are confused as to who's turn it is just click the computer turn this will change it to your turn.

Lion

Health:400
Attack: Lions attack does a great amount of damage.
dodge: This ability does a bit of damage and has a chance to make
your opponents next turn miss you if it's an attack against you.
Bask: Heals you for a moderate amount and increases your Attack
ability damage. This ability stacks.

Turtle

Health:500
Attack: Does an average amount of damage.
Shell retreat: Heals a small amount and increases defense a small
amount. This ability stacks.
Snap: This does an extreme amount of damage. But erases your defense
and increases opponents attacks.

Giraffe

Health:600
Attack: Does a small amount of damage.
Flail: Does a moderate amount of damage half of that is inflicted
on self.
